About Me


I was born in Sheffield and moved to Scotland in 2000 and I am currently living in Central Scotland. I recently took up photography as a hobby after I realised I enjoyed trying to capture moments on my mobile phone, and I naturally progressed onto a DSLR and joined a local Camera Club in Clackmannanshire. The club then inspired me to try to improve my photography through a series of talks, presentations and fellow member advice.

My first lens purchase was a wide angle lens for landscape photography as this was my main interest. I quickly became interested in all aspects of photography with wildlife being my current favoured subject.

I am lucky to live in Scotland as it is arguably the best place in the UK for both landscape and wildlife photography, and it also provides a great opportunity to catch the Northern Lights (although I am yet to be lucky enough with the weather and the conditions to get 'That' shot) I love travelling around Scotland with my partner and enjoying all that this beautiful country has to offer. I also love travelling and I take the opportunity to try to capture the special moments on camera. I am also a regular visitor to Australia and I aim to capture it's landscape and wildlife on my travels.
